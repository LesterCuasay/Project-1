Different Workouts

Weight Training

Weight Training is a popular way of training to increase strength, the size of the skeletal muscles and maintance of strength.

Cardio

Cardio exercises are vast ranging from walking, running, cycling. Any exercise that gets your heart rate up will work your respiratory system and train it.

Crossfit

Crossfit is a form of High Intensity Interval Training (HIIT for short). This workout trains strength and conditioning together with functional movement done in high intensity.

Stretching

Stretching helps maintain flexibility, which helps your body perform different workouts.
